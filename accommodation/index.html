ACCOMMODATION

Conference Hotel

The 14th ACM International Conference on Distributed and Event-Based Systems has arranged special prices for accommodation. The Alt hotel located near to the conference venue, has special offers from July 12th to 18th, 2020 for the DEBS participants. To make the reservation, call 1 855 823 8120 by mentioning DEBS Association for Computing Machinery. The other option is to make the online reservation by mentioning the following block code: 2007ASSOCI.
